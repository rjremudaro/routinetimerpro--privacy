Privacy Policy for RoutineTimer Pro

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

RoutineTimer Pro ("the App") is developed by Moedly. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

RoutineTimer Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you create in the App (routines, drills, streaks, completion history) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

No data is sent to any server, third party, or analytics service.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time In-App Purchase ("Pro") processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@moedly.com
